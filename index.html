Our Mission

The Division of Business and Finance is responsible for a wide range of services to support academic and administrative functions at Wiley University. Our responsibilities include:

  • Accounting and Financial Reporting
  • Budget Development and Management
  • Fiscal Compliance and Internal Controls
  • Auxiliary Services Management
  • Facilities Operations and Maintenance
  • Information Technology Services
  • Risk Management and Insurance
  • Student Financial Services

Auxiliary Services

Business Office

Accounts payable, payroll, purchasing, travel reimbursement, and vendor management.

Facilities Management

Financial Aid

Grants, loans, scholarships, work-study, and financial counseling services.

Information Technology

Help desk, email, WiFi, computer labs, software, and technical support.

Risk Management

Insurance, liability, safety compliance, and incident reporting.

Student Accounts

Tuition billing, payment plans, refunds, and 1098-T tax forms.

Transportation & Fleet

Parking permits, shuttle services, fleet vehicles, and citation appeals.
