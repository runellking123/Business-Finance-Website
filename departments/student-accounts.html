About Student Accounts

The Student Accounts office manages all student billing, payment processing, and financial account services. We work to provide clear, accurate billing information and flexible payment options to help students meet their financial obligations and focus on their academic success.

Our services include tuition billing, payment plan enrollment, refund processing, third-party billing coordination, and 1098-T tax form distribution. Visit us in the King Administration Building or contact us at (903) 927-4200 with questions about your student account.

Our Services

Tuition Billing

E-bills are sent to your Wiley email each semester when tuition is posted. View your current balance and itemized charges through the MyWiley Portal under Student Accounts.

Payment Plans

Divide your balance into monthly installments with a small enrollment fee. Enroll through MyWiley Portal or submit a Payment Plan Enrollment form before the payment deadline.

Refunds

Credit balance refunds are processed within 7-10 business days after aid disburses. Direct deposit is 2-3 days faster than paper checks. Enroll in direct deposit for refunds.

1098-T Tax Forms

Tax forms are available in MyWiley Portal by January 31st each year. Consent to electronic delivery for faster access. Used for education tax credits on your federal return.

Third-Party Billing

Employers, sponsors, and military benefits can be billed directly with proper authorization. Submit the Third-Party Billing Authorization form with sponsor documentation.

Account Holds

Holds may result from unpaid balances, missing documents, or parking citations. Holds prevent registration, transcripts, and graduation. Contact our office to resolve.

Frequently Asked Questions

Log in to the MyWiley Portal and navigate to Student Accounts > View Bill. You can see your current balance, payment history, and itemized charges. E-bills are sent to your Wiley email each semester when tuition is posted. You can also authorize parents or sponsors to view your bill.

Payment deadlines vary by semester; see the Payment Deadlines table above for specific dates. Full payment or an approved payment plan must be in place by the deadline to avoid late fees and potential course drops. Financial aid pending on your account counts toward this requirement.

Enroll in a payment plan through the MyWiley Portal or submit a Payment Plan Enrollment form. Plans divide your balance into monthly installments with a small enrollment fee. You must enroll before the payment deadline each semester. Automatic payments from a bank account or credit card are available.

1098-T tax forms are available in the MyWiley Portal under Student Accounts > Tax Information by January 31st each year. You can consent to receive it electronically using the 1098-T Consent Form for faster access. Paper forms are mailed to your address on file if you don't consent to electronic delivery.

Refunds from financial aid credit balances are processed within 7-10 business days after aid disburses to your account. Direct deposit refunds arrive 2-3 days faster than paper checks. Enroll in Direct Deposit for Refunds to receive your money quickly and securely.
