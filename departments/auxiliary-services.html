About Auxiliary Services

Auxiliary Services supports the Wiley University campus community by providing essential services that enhance student life and campus operations. Our department oversees the campus bookstore, dining services, Wildcat ID cards, mail services, vending operations, and laundry facilities.

We are committed to delivering high-quality services that meet the daily needs of students, faculty, and staff while contributing to a vibrant campus experience. Our team works to ensure that all auxiliary operations run efficiently and support the university's mission of academic excellence.

Our Services

Campus Bookstore

The bookstore offers textbooks, school supplies, Wiley merchandise, and course materials. Order textbooks online for pickup or delivery, and shop for official Wildcat apparel and gifts.

Dining Services

Campus dining options include the main dining hall and convenience locations. Meal plans are required for residential students. Flex dollars can be used at all campus dining locations.

Wildcat ID Card

Your Wildcat ID provides campus access, meal plan usage, library services, and event entry. Report lost cards immediately to protect your account. Replacement cards are $25.

Mail Services

The Campus Mail Center receives packages and mail for students. You'll receive email notification when packages arrive. Bring your Wildcat ID for pickup. Packages held for 14 days.

Vending Services

Vending machines are located in residence halls and campus buildings. Report malfunctions or refund requests to the Auxiliary Services office.

Laundry Services

Laundry facilities are available in each residence hall. Report maintenance issues to Facilities Management. Most machines accept card payment through your Wildcat ID.

Frequently Asked Questions

Meal plan changes can be made during the first two weeks of each semester. Submit a Meal Plan Change Request form online or visit our office. Changes after the deadline require approval from the Director of Auxiliary Services and may be subject to prorated charges.

Replacement ID cards cost $25. If your card was lost or stolen, report it immediately so we can deactivate it and protect any funds on your account. Bring a valid photo ID to our office to receive your replacement card, typically issued the same day.

Packages are delivered to the Campus Mail Center. You'll receive an email notification when your package arrives. Bring your Wildcat ID card to pick it up during mail center hours. Packages are held for 14 days before being returned to sender.

Yes, Flex Dollars on your Wildcat ID can be used at participating off-campus merchants. Look for the "Wildcat Card Accepted Here" sign. A list of participating vendors is available on our website and updated each semester.

Dining hours are reduced during fall, winter, spring, and summer breaks. Limited service is typically available at the main dining hall. Check the Dining Services website or the campus app for updated hours during breaks. Students remaining on campus should plan accordingly.
