About the Business Office

The Business Office at Wiley University is responsible for managing the university's financial operations, ensuring fiscal accountability and supporting the institution's mission. Our team handles accounts payable, payroll processing, purchasing, travel reimbursement, and vendor management, providing essential financial services to students, faculty, staff, and external partners.

We are committed to maintaining financial integrity through sound internal controls and transparent processes. The Business Office is located in the King Administration Building and serves as a resource for departments across campus with procurement, payment processing, and financial compliance needs. Contact us at (903) 927-4200 or visit during regular business hours.

Our Services

Accounts Payable

We process vendor invoices, employee reimbursements, and all university payments. Submit invoices with proper approvals to ensure timely payment processing within established deadlines.

Payroll Services

Payroll is managed through the Paycom system. Employees are classified as exempt or nonexempt per FLSA guidelines. Nonexempt employees must submit accurate time reports for pay calculations. Access pay stubs and W-4 information through Paycom.

Purchasing

We manage procurement services including purchase orders, vendor registration, and competitive bidding for purchases over $5,000. Purchases over $500 require a PO; smaller purchases may use P-Cards.

Travel Reimbursement

Submit travel expenses with original receipts using the Travel Reimbursement Request form. Pre-approval is required for university travel. Reimbursements are processed within 10-14 business days.

Petty Cash

Petty cash funds are available for small, incidental purchases under $100. Submit a Petty Cash Request with proper documentation and supervisor approval. Funds must be reconciled monthly.

Tax Services

W-2 forms are distributed by January 31st annually. 1099 forms are issued to qualifying vendors and contractors. Access tax documents through the Paycom portal or contact our office for duplicates.

Frequently Asked Questions

Payroll is processed monthly and bi-weekly, depending on employee classification. Monthly employees are paid on the last business day of each month. Bi-weekly employees are paid every other Friday. Employees enrolled in direct deposit typically see funds in their accounts on the scheduled pay date. Paper checks can be picked up at the Business Office after 2:00 PM on paydays.

New vendors must complete a W-9 form and Vendor Registration form. Submit these to the Business Office along with any required insurance certificates. Allow 5-7 business days for processing. Once approved, you'll be added to our vendor database and can receive purchase orders.

Travel reimbursements are typically processed within 10-14 business days of receiving a complete Travel Reimbursement Request with all required receipts. Incomplete submissions will be returned, which may delay processing. Direct deposit reimbursements are faster than paper checks.

W-2 forms are mailed to your address on file by January 31st each year. You can also access your W-2 electronically through the MyWiley Portal under Employee Self-Service. If you need a duplicate, contact the Business Office with your employee ID.

Purchases over $500 require a purchase order (PO). For purchases under $500, departments may use a purchasing card (P-Card) or submit a petty cash request. All purchases over $5,000 require competitive bidding. Contact Purchasing for guidance on larger acquisitions.
