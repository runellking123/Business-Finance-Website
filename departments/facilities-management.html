About Facilities Management

The Facilities Management department is responsible for maintaining Wiley University's physical campus infrastructure, ensuring that all buildings, grounds, and systems operate safely and efficiently. Our dedicated team works around the clock to provide a clean, comfortable, and well-maintained environment that supports the academic mission of the university.

We oversee building maintenance, custodial services, grounds keeping, key control, event setup, and moving services for all campus facilities. Our department is committed to sustainability practices and proactive maintenance to preserve the beauty and functionality of the historic Wiley campus for current and future generations of Wildcats.

Emergency Maintenance: For after-hours emergencies (floods, power outages, safety hazards), call Campus Police at (903) 927-3300

Our Services

Building Maintenance

Our maintenance team handles HVAC systems, plumbing, electrical, carpentry, and general repairs for all campus buildings. Submit a work order for routine repairs or call for emergency service.

Custodial Services

Daily cleaning and sanitation services for all academic buildings, administrative offices, and common areas. Special cleaning requests can be submitted through the work order system.

Grounds Keeping

Landscaping, lawn care, tree maintenance, and outdoor area upkeep to maintain the beauty of our historic campus. Includes snow/ice removal during winter weather events.

Key Services

Building and office key requests must be submitted by department supervisors. Keys are typically ready within 3-5 business days. All keys must be returned when leaving employment.

Event Setup

Room setup, furniture arrangement, and equipment placement for campus events. Submit requests at least 5 business days in advance; complex setups may require 2 weeks notice.

Moving Services

Furniture moving and office relocation assistance for university departments. Submit a moving request form with detailed inventory and timeline requirements.

Submit a Work Order

Need maintenance, custodial, or setup assistance? Submit a work order online and our team will respond promptly.

Frequently Asked Questions

Submit work orders online using the Work Order Request Form on this page, or call our office directly. Include the building name, room number, and a detailed description of the issue. You'll receive a confirmation email with a work order number to track your request.

For after-hours emergencies such as floods, power outages, gas leaks, or safety hazards, call Campus Police at (903) 927-3300. They will dispatch the appropriate emergency maintenance personnel. Do not attempt to address electrical or gas issues yourself.

Key requests must be submitted by your department supervisor using the Key Request Form. Include the building, room number, and business justification. Keys are typically ready within 3-5 business days. All keys must be returned when leaving employment or changing departments.

Submit Space Setup Requests at least 5 business days before your event for standard setups. Complex setups or large events may require 2 weeks notice. Last-minute requests are accommodated when possible but cannot be guaranteed. Include detailed setup requirements in your request.

Report safety hazards immediately by calling our office or submitting an urgent work order. For imminent dangers (gas leaks, electrical hazards, structural issues), evacuate the area and call Campus Police at (903) 927-3300. Document the hazard location and nature for quick response.
