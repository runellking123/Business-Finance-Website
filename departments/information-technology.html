About Information Technology

The Information Technology department provides comprehensive technology services to support the academic mission of Wiley University. We are committed to delivering reliable, secure, and innovative technology solutions that enhance teaching, learning, research, and administrative operations across campus.

Our services include help desk support, email and Microsoft 365 administration, campus WiFi, computer lab management, printing services, and software licensing. We work closely with faculty, staff, and students to ensure technology resources are accessible and effective in supporting academic excellence.

Our Services

Help Desk Support

Submit a help desk ticket online or call for technical assistance. Our team handles hardware issues, software problems, account access, and general tech support.

Email & Microsoft 365

All students and employees receive a Wiley email account and free access to Microsoft 365, including Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Teams, and OneDrive cloud storage.

Campus WiFi

Connect to "WileySecure" using your Wiley username and password. Gaming consoles and smart devices can be registered through the MyWiley Portal.

Computer Labs

Open-access computer labs are available in multiple campus locations with printing, scanning, and specialized software for academic coursework.

Printing Services

Print from any campus computer or personal device using WileyPrint. Pick up prints at any campus printer by swiping your Wildcat ID. Students receive a semester printing allowance.

Software & Licenses

Access free software through the MyWiley Portal including Microsoft 365, antivirus, and program-specific applications. Submit a Software Request for new installations.

Frequently Asked Questions

Select "WileySecure" from available networks and log in with your Wiley username and password. First-time users may need to accept the security certificate. For gaming consoles or smart devices that can't use enterprise WiFi, register your device MAC address through the MyWiley Portal.

Use the Password Reset tool in the MyWiley Portal. You'll need to verify your identity using your security questions or backup email. If you're locked out completely, visit the Help Desk with a valid photo ID. Passwords must be at least 12 characters with mixed case, numbers, and symbols.

Go to outlook.office.com and sign in with your full Wiley email address (username@wileyc.edu) and password. You can also access email through the Outlook mobile app. Your email account includes Microsoft 365 apps like Word, Excel, and Teams at no cost.

Send print jobs to "WileyPrint" from any campus computer or your personal device using the web print portal. Pick up your prints at any campus printer by swiping your Wildcat ID card. Students receive a printing allowance each semester; additional pages can be purchased at Student Accounts.

All students receive free access to Microsoft 365 (Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Teams), plus discipline-specific software varies by program. Visit the Software Downloads page in MyWiley Portal for available titles. Some software requires installation on campus computers; submit a Software Request for new installations.
